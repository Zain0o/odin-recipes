KLADDKAKKA

DESCRIPTION

At the café, we’ve been baking that most famous Swedish cake – Kladdkaka – for years. The name means ‘sticky cake’ – owing to the fact that the middle is left a little under baked and still sticky. There is not baking power in this cake precisely to keep it dense and claggy.

INGREDIENTS

  • 150 g butter 100 g good quality milk chocolate or dark, if you prefer darker flavours 200 g caster sugar 2 eggs 40 g cocoa powder 125 g plain flour 1 tsp vanilla sugar

    • STEPS

    1. Turn the oven to 180C (200c if not fan)
    2. Melt the butter and then add the chocolate – and set aside.
    3. In a stand mixer, whisk the egg and sugar until pale and fluffy.
